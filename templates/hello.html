From the graphs above and other independent data analysis, we believe the demographic factors that most impact customer engagement is household income and age. As customers began to climb in income, they made less purchases. This could be because this organization is targeting a low-income demographic, among other things. Age impacted customer engagement in a sort of bell pattern, with engagement peaking at the 45-55 range and decreasing on either end of that age range. We found that factors such as marital status and household size did not have as much of an impact as the other demographic factors.
